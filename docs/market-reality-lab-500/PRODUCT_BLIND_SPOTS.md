2. Convenience dice ≠ Taiwan dinner reality (hotpot, night market)
3. Mothers need family meal not personal bento
4. Medical-anxiety wants white-coat not emoji
5. Students want social share not science
6. Platform期 silent = product feels broken
7. Paywall blocks charts before user sees value curve
8. No win-back for returners
9. Shift workers need time-axis not label rename
10. 499/500 typo signals「還在試價」not trust
