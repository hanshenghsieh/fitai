# BetterBit Market Reality Lab
**Date:** 2026-06-19  
**Mission:** Kill the product. Reality first.  
**Method:** 100 unique Taiwan lives · D1→D180 · forced 70/20/10 outcome

---

## Executive Verdict

**BetterBit does NOT yet deserve to exist at scale.**

Not because the science is wrong. Because **the market does not pay for correct math it cannot feel within 72 hours**, while free alternatives (ChatGPT, 小紅書, 營養師諮詢一次) deliver *felt* personalization faster.

| Metric (100-user sim) | Result |
|----------------------|--------|
| Quit by D90 | **70** |
| Stay unpaid | **20** |
| Subscribe | **10** |
| Would recommend | **8** |
| Refund within 14 days | **4** of 10 subscribers |

**Subscription rate sensitivity:** 1% @ mass market · 3% @ fitness-intent traffic · 5% @ referred office-worker cohort · 10% only with human onboarding call (not in product today).

**If 10,000 users tomorrow, it dies by:** Week 3 trial cliff → 6,500 never form D7 habit → 2,800 ghost accounts → 400 angry "why 7-11 again?" reviews → Stripe disputes + 小紅書 "智商稅" thread → founder spends all time on support, zero on retention loop.
